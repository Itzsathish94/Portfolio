About me

I worked as a Roller Skating coach for over 9 years,I worked under various sections of skating. Started as a Junior Roller Skating coach, being promoted several times, finally promoted to Roller Hockey Head Coach of Thanjavur Skating Academy. Being a Roller hockey player myself, i won 10 State Roller Hockey Championship as a player and Captained the team. During my tenure as Roller hockey head coach, Thanjavur won Overall State Championship every year.

Then i wanted a career change, so on November 2023 i left Roller Skating in the pursuit of learning a high income skill. I found Brototype through my friend and was immediately hooked. I attended workshop on January 2023 and passed the mock test in 1st attempt. Now i am determined to complete the 28 weeks brocamp programme and land a job as a Full stack developer.
